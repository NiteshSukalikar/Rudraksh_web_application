About Us
Rudralife is a pioneer organisation in the field of Rudraksha. We collect customer experiences and are actively working towards helping more people on their journey towards upliftment.
Our Mission
It is our mission to spread awareness and educate the masses about the divine Rudraksha beads. Our aim is to supply the best quality Nepal Rudraksha to our customers and empower their lives through the power of these gifts of Lord Shiva.
Our Vision
Since 22 years, Rudralife has been working dedicatedly to spread knowledge of Rudraksha to the masses. We have our own ISO certified laboratories for Rudraksha testing. We are the world's largest organisation of Rudraksha due to our emphasis on quality, credibility and collecting customer experiences.
